About Us


Keansburg park is a park that features various attractions, such as rides and games, as well as other events for entertainment purposes. In 1904, William Gehlhaus convinced five investors to join him in purchasing an area of marshland overlooking Raritan Bay, in hopes of creating a resort area. In 1909, Gehlhaus founded the Keansburg Steamboat Company to bring visitors across the bay from New York City. The borough of Keansburg, which was incorporated in 1917, basically grew up around William Gehlhaus' vision of summertime fun. And 110 years later, Keansburg, "the gem of the Bayshore," is still best known for its namesake amusement park. Keansburg Amusement Park now offers over 40 rides including kiddie family and thrill rides. It is our commitment to ensuring all of our guests have a safe and enjoyable visit.
